## Why SUPER Skills For AI Vibe Coding

Super Product Business Finance gives AI coding agents business judgement for product decisions. Use it for market sizing, startup analysis, pricing, packaging, monetisation, roadmap prioritisation, metrics, and decision logs.

These skills are designed for **AI vibe coding**: fast, agent-assisted building where the AI needs strong domain context, practical workflows, guardrails, and implementation-ready outputs.

**SEO and discovery keywords:** product management, business analysis, market sizing, startup analysis, pricing strategy, monetisation, PM frameworks, roadmap prioritisation, AI vibe coding.

## Install

Copy this folder into your agent's skills directory, then restart or reload the agent.

```bash
cp -R super-product-business-finance ~/.your-agent/skills/
```

Use it by name:

```text
Use $super-product-business-finance to help with this request.
```
