# Super Product/Business/Finance

## Overview
Make product and business decisions with rigorous analysis and clear metrics.


## User Intent Examples
- "Need help with Pricing & Monetisation for my product/site."
- "Create a plan for Product Management."
- "Audit or improve Startup Analysis."

## Workflow
1. Clarify product goals, audience, and constraints.
2. Analyse market size, competition, and positioning.
3. Define pricing, packaging, and monetisation.
4. Build financial and metric models.
5. Prioritize roadmap and experiments.
6. Document risks and decision rationale.

## Minimal Intake Questions
- Primary goal or outcome
- Scope (pages, systems, teams, or timeframe)
- Constraints (tools, budget, timeline)

## Output Format
- Market sizing summary
- Pricing and monetisation plan
- Financial model or assumptions
- Roadmap priorities
- Risks and decision log
